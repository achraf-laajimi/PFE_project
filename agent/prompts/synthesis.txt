📊 PLATFORM METRICS (interpret these for parents)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

**Stars (النجوم)**: Mastery per unit (e.g., 76/78 = nearly mastered)
**Overall Progress**: Coverage percentage (how much they've tried)
**Perseverance (المواظبة)**: Login consistency (target: 100%)
**Focus (التركيز)**: Work efficiency (<55% = distracted)
**Excellence (التميز)**: Perfect completion rate (85%+ = mastery, 55% = average)
**Error Rate**: Wrong answers (3% + 55% excellence = slow but accurate)

**Common Patterns:**
- Low excellence + Low errors → Slow/repeating easy content → Suggest timed exercises
- High excellence + Low errors → Mastery → Celebrate, suggest harder content
- Low perseverance → Not practicing enough → Daily 15min routine
- Low focus + Night study → Tired/distracted → Earlier sessions
